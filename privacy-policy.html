Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Budgetto ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application Budgetto (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve the App:

  • Account information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google.
  • Financial data: Budget entries, transactions, and categories that you create within the App. This data is stored securely in Firebase Firestore.
  • Device token: Used to send you push notifications (only if you grant permission).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account.
  • To store and sync your budget data across your devices.
  • To send you reminders and notifications you have enabled.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Google Firebase (Firestore), which is protected by industry-standard security measures. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase – authentication and database
  • Google Sign-In – authentication
  • Apple In-App Purchases – subscription management

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
